Rules:

  1. Press Any Key of Your Keyboard to Start the Game.
  2. Click the Colour that Beep.
  3. Remember the Previous Colours.
  4. Now Click the Colours Sequencially.
  5. If the Sequence is Wrong, Game Will be Restarted.

Example:

  • Suppose 1st Beep Red. Click Red.
  • Then Beep Blue. Click Red ➡️ Blue.
  • Then Beep Green. Click Red ➡️ Blue ➡️ Green.
  • Then Beep Yellow. Click Red ➡️ Blue ➡️ Green ➡️ Yellow.

Note: Number of Level Indicates How Many Times You Have to Click.
